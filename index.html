Recycled Products Brand India · Waste Management
0 → 30K followers 4 videos × 1M+ views
+

Context

Sub-brand of a waste management company with no dedicated social media presence.

Challenge

Low brand awareness and limited visibility in a crowded market.

Approach

  • Awareness-led strategy rooted in responsible messaging
  • Positioned products as alternatives, not purchases
  • Simplified sustainability narratives for broader reach

Results

  • 0 → 30K Instagram followers in 3 years
  • 4 videos crossed 1M+ views
  • Increased inbound leads

Positioning the product as a conscious alternative, rather than pushing it as a purchase, built trust and translated into higher-quality engagement.
